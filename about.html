About us



Living Faith Church Makurdi, aka Winners Chapel is a branch of Living Faith Church Worldwide. It is the State Headquarters of Living Faith Churches in Benue State, overseeing the over 500 ever growing Churches spread across the state. It runs three power packed services every Sunday morning.

THE MANDATE

"The hour has come to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the WORD of FAITH and I am sending you to undertake this task." (Delivered 2nd May, 1981).

Living Faith Church Worldwide a.k.a Winners' Chapel International is a christian denomination whose primary objective is the development and upliftment of mankind and also stirring up the God given potentials embeded in people of all races and nations. Our task is to liberate the entire world from all oppressions of the enemy both spiritual and physical through the teachings and preaching of the word of Faith, schooling them to exploits in life and releasing men and women to their full potentials to benefit humanity. For more than two decades, Dr. David O. Oyedepo, has been part of the current charismatic renaissance overwhelming the world. His faith-based teaching has impacted millions of lives, called with a specific mandate to liberate mankind from all oppression.

The commission is associated with the Communion of Bible Faith Ministries, dedicated to the empowerment of the total man and the discipline of character building in this high velocity society. It seeks to provide a compass setting for the making of responsible citizens and leaders of men in every nation by equipping them through training programs, materials as well as financial support. We have through the grace given us of God, living proofs that this task is progressing with tremendous success by the testimonies of dramatic changes and improvement in the quality of life of the people every where our organisation is represented.

The teaching of the Church has been categorized into what we refer to as the 12 pillars of the Commision (Faith, The Word, Prayer, Consecration, Prosperity, Vision, Wisdom, Success, Sign and Wonders, The Holy Spirit, Divine Healing and Praise) which forms the basis of the prophetic focus of each month of the Year. The detailed summary of the Ministry is documented in our operation manual called The Mandate which documents who we are, what we believe, the vision, what we do, code of conduct for our operations and operators.

ORGANISATIONAL STRUCTURE:

Living Faith Church Worldwide through it's missions arm: World Mission Agency (WMA) which provides employment opportunities wherever it exists, has a total staff strength that stands at over two thousand employees in Nigeria alone. This organisation which is made up of dynamic professionals has as its Chairman, Dr. David O. Oyedepo (Ph.D). He is also the Presiding Bishop of the Living Faith Church worldwide a.k.a Winners Chapel with a network of churches all over Nigeria and most nations of Africa, Europe and USA. He is also the Senior Pastor of Faith Tabernacle, the 50,000-seater sanctuary reported to be the largest church auditorium in the world. It also stands as one of the largest single congregation in the world.
